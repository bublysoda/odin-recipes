Cubed Cinnamon Apples

This healthy snack is a hit for everyone!

Ingredients:

  • One apple per person
  • Cinnamon, to taste
  • Butter, to taste

Steps:

  1. Cube the apples. For additional instructions on how to cut an apple into cubes, you can visit this link for more info
  2. Grab a stick of butter and cut as much butter is needed to grease the pan and the apples
  3. Turn on the stovetop with a frying pan on top
  4. Put the butter and apple cubes in a frying pan, and stir until apples are covered in butter
  5. Coat the apples in cinnamon to taste
  6. Stir until the cinnamon sticks to the cubes
  7. Enjoy

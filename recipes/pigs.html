Meat Lovers Pigs in a Blanket

This sweet and smokey pigs in a blanket recipe will be a guaranteed smash hit for all of your party members!

Ingredients:

  • Toothpicks
  • Brown sugar
  • Bacon
  • Cocktail Weenies

Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees
  2. Cut the bacon in half or thirds depending on how thick you want them
  3. Wrap the bacon around the cocktail weenies
  4. Stick the bacon to the cocktail weenie by implaing it with the toothpick
  5. Liberally coat the the wrapped weenie with brown sugar
  6. Put the pigs in a blanket on a baking sheet and set in the oven for 20 minutes
  7. Remove from oven and enjoy!
